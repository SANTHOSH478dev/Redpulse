Blood Donation Awareness

Learn, understand, and spread awareness about the importance of blood donation.

Awareness

Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation saves lives during surgeries, accidents, childbirth, cancer treatments, and medical emergencies.

Eligibility

Who Can Donate Blood?

Healthy individuals aged 18+ with minimum weight and good health can donate blood safely.

Safety

Is Blood Donation Safe?

Yes. Blood donation is completely safe when performed at authorized hospitals and blood banks.

Myths

Common Myths About Donation

Donating blood does not weaken the body permanently. The body replenishes donated blood quickly.

Care

After Donation Care

Drink fluids, rest for a short time, and avoid heavy physical activity immediately after donating blood.

Impact

One Donation, Many Lives

A single blood donation can save up to three lives. Your small act can make a big difference.
